Hotel Description

For the travelers who are traveling to Kedarnath and are in search of a comfy accommodation that is easy on pocket, Hotel Punjab Sindh Awas is an ideal option. It is a relaxing budget category hotel located at the exquisite Kedarnath Town in close vicinity to the renowned Kedarnath Temple. The hotel offers good value of the money with the facilities and services like doctor on call, travel desk, car parking facility, round the clock reception, electricity backup, and more.

The hotel renders a congenial and convenient accommodation in its double and four bedded family rooms that are tastefully and elegantly designed and well furnished with modern amenities to cater a comfortable sojourn to the guests. Some of the room amenities are attached bathroom, comfy beds, round the clock room service, running water, and more. The hotel serves a potpourri of hot and lip smacking pure vegetarian meals.
