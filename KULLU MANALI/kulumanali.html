Tour Itinerary:

Day-01: Chandigarh/Delhi — Manali

Service: Volvo Only-- This is an overnight journey and it takes around 12-14 hours. You can board a Volvo from Delhi. The tourists will have a comfortable overnight journey by the Volvo.
On your way to Manali, you are going to experience the exotic beauty and the picturesque views that are worth watching. The cool breeze of Manali also amazes the tourists and takes their stress away.

Day-02: Manali — Local-Sightseeing

Arrival at Manali in the morning. You will be Picked up from the Manali bus stand & Then Check-in at the hotel at 11 Noon. In the Afternoon you can go for a half-day local city tour of - "Hadimba Temple" built in 1553 with a superbly crafted pagoda roof, it is famous for its exquisitely carved doorway. "Club House" with comprehensive facilities that include roller skating, an auditorium, billiards room, a library, a bar, and a restaurant makes a wonderful outing for the day.

And tourist also visit -- "Tibetan Monastery", "Van Vihar", "Manu Temple", "Jogini WaterFall" and “Vashist Temple” are well known for their Hot springs. There are old temples dedicated to the Saga Vashit and Lord Rama.

After you explore these places, in the evening visit “Mall Road” and return to the hotel, have dinner. Overnight stay at the hotel in Manali.

Day-03: Manali — Solang Valley — Atal Tunnel

Morning, after breakfast proceed for a full day sightseeing in :- Solang Valley, Kothi Village, Nehru Kund, Him-Valley, Apple Garden, Shooting Place, While visiting - "Solang Valley" one should also try their hands-on adventure activities as it is one of the best places for adventures near Manali. You can enjoy activities such as Zorbing, Snow Scooter, Horse Riding, Paragliding, Skiing, and a lot more. And also can enjoy a short Trek to “Anjani Mahadev”. Overnight stay at a hotel in Manali.

The tourists also go to the :- “Rohtang Pass” that is near only and experience the snow-scaped mountains. Go through “Atal Tunnel” and also visit Gulaba.

Please Note: Since the National Green Tribunal is allowing only limited vehicles to Rohtang pass per day. Rohtang pass will be subject to availability of permit on the spot with additional cost on sharing basis or individual basis as per guest requirement & Rohtang is closed on every Tuesday.

Day-04: Manali — Kullu — Manikaran -- Kasol

On the next day, the morning after breakfast, tourists can visit the Pilgrimage – Manikaran Sahib Gurdwara-(Natural Hot Spring Water) and enjoy the hot water springs that have medical properties & take the blessings of God/goodness. The tourists can explore the nearby places like -- Kasol, Kasol Market, Shiv Temple, Flea Market, Parvati Valley, and they can even do trekking.

Tourists also visit :- Kullu, Vaishno Devi Temple, Kullu Market, Kullu Valley, Rabbit farm & River Rafting – River Rafting is also very famous around there and visit the Handicrafts and Shawls Factory. Later, return to the hotel and have your dinner. Overnight stay at the hotel in Manali.

Day-05: Manali — Delhi/Chandigarh Departure – (Overnight Journey)

The departure journey will be overnight as well. The couple can check out at the hotel by 11 am and do some Shopping on Mall Road on their own. In the evening at 06 PM board the Volvo AC bus for Delhi for the overnight journey.

Drop from Hotel to Manali Bus Stand by Taxi will be provided by us. Board your Volvo to get back to your hometown. For the departure as well the tourist can book from the customizable options that are available in the package. The hotels, food, sports, and destinations are according to their choice and preferences.

Day-06: Reach Back Delhi Around 8 Am

On your 6th day, the tourists will reach Delhi by 8am in the morning.
